About Me

Hi! My name is Kade Hasenfus, and I am currently a senior at UMass Lowell majoring in Computer Science. I first got interested in the field of Computer Science at a young age, when I first started gaining interest in video games. As I grew older and became even more involved with video games and consequently Computer Science, I developed an interest in Computer Science all on its own. Beginning with hosting a Minecraft server on my laptop for friends in 2010, I have since been expanding my knowledge in my free time in areas such as networking and systems administration, to experience with hardware both in the consumer and enterprise space. Currently, I host numerous game servers and other services on a Dell Poweredge R710 I aquired second hand from ebay.

As for my interests outside the field, I enjoy biking, swimming, hiking, wakeboarding, target shooting, and skeet shooting, just to name a few.
